In answer to your questions we have managed to determine the following from audit data:

Counter 1 was used for balancing the stock unit during the morning. It froze at 12:30 because of a message
store lock problem. User phoned up the help desk (call logged 12:45) and was advised to reboot, which they
did at 12:53. Counter used successfully during the afternoon.

A user started to log on to Counter 2 at 8:40am but this was not successful, and it is not clear what state it was
in. This counter was also rebooted at 12:53, although not reported to the helpdesk. It was used successfully
from 13:20 onwards.

There is no indication whatsoever of a software upgrade on 1/4. Upgrades are done overnight, not during the
working day.
