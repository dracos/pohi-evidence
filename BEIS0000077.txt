Annex B: Personnel changes in POL’s Board and Group Executive over the last 3 years

The tables below highlight new members of POL’s Board and Group Executive over the past three
years. Two Executives — the CEO and CFO - typically sit on both the Board and GE. However, at
present the CFO position is vacant so Al Cameron is the only Executive currently sitting on the
Board.

Board level changes

Only two new people have joined the Board in the last three years: the Shareholder NED and the
Shareholder Representative NED. However, Ministers have reappointed a further three members,
all within the last year, including the Chair. In addition, while Al Cameron has been on the Board
as CFO since 2015, he was appointed as interim CEO on 1* April 2019.
