Thank you for the opportunity to attend this session on the Post Office mediation scheme

The Scheme was set up in 2013 to give the small number of people with complaints about
our system a chance to make their case - and for each case to be thoroughly investigated

and independently reviewed.

We have now investigated all of the cases put to us. It has taken longer than we would have
liked but a thorough approach has been essential. The Scheme continues through the

independent review of cases along with mediation where appropriate.

We have approached the scheme with the utmost seriousness and rigour. Our investigation
of cases has reinforced our confidence that the Horizon system works as it should. The Post
Office is part of the fabric of the nation, with 11,500 branches, most of them small

businessmen and women, serving 17m people every week.

Thanks to the rigour of the work that has been done, we can continue to be confident in our
system and continue to improve in areas where we can do better for the people who work in

our branches.
