Post Office
Investigation Second Sight’s Horizon review

Scope

1. Review the events leading up to the appointment of Second Sight with particular
focus on the Post Office response to James Arbuthnot’s suggestion that there should
be an independent review

a. Consider why these claims had not been investigated previously

2. Review the process of the appointment of Second Sight (scope, output, fees etc)

3. Review the PO management of the Second Sight work and identify lessons to be
learnt with clear recommendations for future similar reviews

a. To included specific focus on the management of the various external and
stakeholders including (but not limited to) — Shex, JFSA, James Arbuthnot, the
Post Office Board

4, Identify any further immediate actions which we could take in supporting the
conclusion of the current Horizon review

5. Consider and wider implications from the Second Sight review work including
cultural, management and process
