POLB03/39 BUSINESS CASES - RULES OVERVIEW

Peter Corbett outlined the revised procedure for approval of
future Company investment cases. The Board noted that:

(a) Business cases below £10m would be approved by the Post
Office Ltd Board rather than the Royal Mail Management
Board. Business cases above £10m would require approval of
the Royal Mail Holdings Board;

(b) An obligation existed for the appropriate papers to be
submitted to Martin Gafsen’s Investment Appraisal team two
weeks before the intended circulation date of the papers to the
Board. Exceptions to this policy would only be made in special
circumstances;

(c) It would be the responsibility of the sponsor to ensure that the
business case was properly written and complete;
