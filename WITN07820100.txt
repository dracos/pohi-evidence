POST OFFICE HORIZON IT INQUIRY

FIRST WITNESS STATEMENT OF KIM RILEY

RILEY, will say as follows...

INTRODUCTION

itr

This witness statement is made to assist the Post Office Horizon IT Inquiry
with the matters set out in the Rule 9 Request dated 13 January 2023.

I worked at Rotherham Post Office for 24 years and during this employment
was seconded to the Field Support Team for around 10 months to support
sub postmasters moving from the old accounting systems to the new Horizon
system.

During my time on this project I saw, heard or came across nothing untoward
or worthy of note to help with this Inquiry.

Statement of Truth

I believe the content of this statement to be true.
