Dear all

As you know, BBC Panorama broadcast its programme on the Horizon system last
night.

The programme was closely monitored by my team, legal and our external advisers.
We are meeting today to assess next steps in relation to the content. My overall view
was that it offered no new revelation and had been weakened in its intent by our
rebuttal over recent weeks, but was very partial, selective and one-sided.

I am glad to say that so far there has been minimal pick up of the issue in the wider
media: it features briefly on BBC news bulletins early evening yesterday, was on the
BBC website overnight as story number 9 in the online running order and has now
been removed from the main news lists. There was a small sidebar piece in the Times.

A team was monitoring social media live in the office last night. While sentiment was
adverse overall, there was a much smaller amount of activity compared to the BBC
documentary last week.

We have plans in place for wider next steps in relation to media, Parliament and
ministerial handling, as well as with wider stakeholders such as the NFSP (whose
view on the issue was not included in the programme). We are reassessing and
refining those plans today.

Thope this is helpful.

Best wishes

Mark
