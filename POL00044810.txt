Astwood Bank Post Office® - Julian WILSON

The loss to Post Office® Ltd in this case was £27,811.28 which related to an audit shortage identified on the
11" September 2008 at Astwood Bank Branch Post Office® (346/246). During PACE interviews with Post
Office® Ltd Investigators, Mr Wilson denied theft but made admissions to falsifying the office accounts over a 5
year period.

A Proceeds of Crime Act (POCA) 2002 Financial Investigation was initiated with a restraint order against the
suspects identified assets being obtained on the 17"” December 2008 at Croydon Crown Court.

A Post Office® prosecution ensued and on the 15"" June 2009 at Worcester Crown Court, the defendant pleaded
guilty to 2 Fraud Act charges. The case was adjourned for sentencing to the 3™ August 2009, where on that
date; the defendant was sentenced to 200 hours Community Service. A POCA 2002 confiscation timetable was
also set at this time.

On the 14" December 2009 at Hereford Crown Court, the POCA 2002 confiscation investigation was concluded
