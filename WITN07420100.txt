POST OFFICE HORIZON IT INQUIRY

FIRST WITNESS STATEMENT OF SUSAN HOWARD

1, SUSAN HOWARD, will say as follows...

4. This witness statement is made to assist the Post Office Horizon IT Inquiry
with the matters set out in the Rule 9 Request dated 26 January 2023.

2. | was a sub postmaster between 1989 and 1991 (approximately) at Oldcotes
Post Office, Worksop, Nottinghamshire. | was a relief counter clerk for GT
News based at Dinnington Post Office. | was a counter clerk for Martins
newsagents, Worksop (between 1993 and 2001 approximately). | was
Manager at Hartley Brook Road Post Office, Sheffield for GT News. | was
Assistant Manager at Dinnington Post Office for GT News. | was then a Senior
Advisor at Network Business Support Centre (‘NBSC”), Brampton (between
approximately 2001 and 2006). | was a Customer Care Officer at NBSC Brampton.
Retired in 2006, when | took redundancy.

3. | think | received my Horizon training whilst | was a relief counter clerk at
Dinnington Post Office and also as part of my induction training for the NBSC.

4, When working in the NBSC | was employed as a Senior Advisor POL and
later as a Customer Care Officer by POL/Royal Mail. If Tier 1 Advisors could

not answer the postmaster's enquiry they transferred the call through to a
