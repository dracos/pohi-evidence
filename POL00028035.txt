Travel Money Card

Every branch can sell a Travel Money Card for home delivery.
Customers can purchase the Travel Money Card in branch and it will
be delivered to their home address in 2-3 working days. All home
delivery Travel Money Cards are personalised with the customer's
name printed on the front and are sent by Royal Mail Ist class delivery.
