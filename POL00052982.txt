POST OFFICE LIMITED v SUZANNE LESLEY PALMER
SOUTHEND CROWN COURT.

TRIAL - 24™ - 26™ JANUARY 2007

CASE NO: POLTD/0506/0619

The above named Defendant appeared at Southend Crown Court between the
24" and 26" January 2007 for trial.

Having heard both the Prosecution and Defence case the Jury retired to
consider their verdict following which they found the Defendant Not Guilty in

relation to all 3 counts on the Indictment.

The Defendant was discharged and no order was made in respect of costs for
either side.

This now concludes the matter.
