BACKGROUND

(A) POL and the entered into a grant framework agreement (Grant Agreement) on 21 July 2015 with
the National Federation of Sub-Postmasters (an unincorporated association) (Outgoing Party).

(B) The Grant Agreement was amended and novated to the NFSP by a deed of novation and
variation between POL, the Outgoing Party and the NFSP and dated 5 September 2016
(Novation Agreement).

(C) The parties wish to further amend the Grant Agreement as set out in this deed with effect from
the date of this deed (Effective Date).

AGREED TERMS
41. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION
