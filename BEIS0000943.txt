Lead private secretary:
Submission title: FOR DECISION: Horizon Shortfall Scheme Appeals Mechanism

Timing: A decision is required urgently to ensure that an announcement can be made ahead
of the next Inquiry hearings in September, illustrating HMG’s proactiveness and a willingness
to action the recommendations of the Horizon Compensation Advisory Board and the
Business and Trade Select Committee.

Summary: This note recommends introducing an independent, government-administered
appeals process for the Horizon Shortfall Scheme (HSS).

Recommendation: That Ministers agree to officials:

+ Developing a government resourced and administered independent appeals process
for the HSS with the aim to introduce this as quickly as possible.

* Working with HM Treasury to obtain funding for this process.

+ Aiming to make an announcement on launching a scheme ahead of the Post Office IT
Inquiry hearings in September 2024.

Annexes
Annex A: Background information on the HSS and criticisms it has faced
Annex B: Financial Estimates and Risks associated with Option 1 — additional information
