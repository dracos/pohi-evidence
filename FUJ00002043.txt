Repair Service Levels.

are classified as Remote. All other postal zone locations are classified as Local.

3 Calculating Service Level Performance

Counter Equipment and LAN related Incidents which prevent an individual Branch from using HNG-X
Services shall be resolved in accordance with SLTs as defined in the CCD entitled: “Engineering
Service: Service Description” (SVM/SDM/SD/0002). Fault priority is allocated to these Incidents
following the receipt of calls to the Service Desk as follows:

e Calls from Local designated Branches will be allocated priority A, B or C in accordance with
the Hardware component failure as defined in the CCD entitled: “Engineering Service:
Service Description” (SVM/SDM/SD/0002);

e Calls from Remote designated Branches will be allocated priority C.
