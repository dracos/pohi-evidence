Annex 3 - Summary Chronology of Key Events

The Inquiry requested a high-level chronology of the various steps POL has taken in relation to providing disclosure to the Inquiry. The body of the
Second Interim Disclosure Statement provides more granular detail. The below table is provided to assist the Inquiry in understanding the dates on which
key events took place.

No. Date Action

1. January 2020 to March 2020 Peters & Peters commence a Post-Conviction Disclosure Exercise ("PCDE Review

2. September 2020 Following receipt of Data Subject Access Requests ("DSARs"), POL identified certain issues
relating to the way in which its historical documents were being stored at Winchester Oasis.
This was initially referred to as the '31,000 boxes’ exercise.

3. 19 and 23 October 2020 4,766 items identified at Winchester Oasis site as being 'unindexed' during the PCDE Review.

4. November 2020 HMC action plan put into place to address the 4,766 unindexed items.

5. November 2020 to January 2021 HSF carry out sampling exercise of the 4,766 unindexed items.

6. In and around 26 January 2021 The HSF sampling exercise completes.

7. 10 February 2021 POL stakeholders updated as to the outcome of the sampling exercise.

8. May 2021 HMC made the decision to complete a pilot exercise to inform stakeholders as to the content
of the 4,766 unindexed boxes. This involved 5% (237) of the 4,766 unindexed items being
indexed ("Pilot Exercise").

9. 1 June 2021 Statutory Inquiry commences.

10. November 2021 Pilot Exercise concluded.

11. 13 December 2021 First Rule 9 Request received that specifically engages phase 2 of the Inquiry, and, as such,
concerned potentially responsive material stored in hard copy document repositories.

12. 27 January 2022 HMC decided that a project should be initiated to index the remaining boxes in the 4,766
unindexed items.
