REGINA v TAHIR MAHMOOD.
BIRMINGHAM CROWN COURT.
SENTENCE - 12TH DECEMBER 2005
CASE NO: 0506/0061

The above case was heard at Birmingham Crown Court on the
17" November 2005. Mr Richard Cole of Counsel attended to prosecute.

The Defendant pleaded Guilty to the 6 specimen counts and accepted the
loss of £33,000 represented by the TIC’s.

The TIC schedule does however have to be amended by the Officer to
include the figures which were stated on the balance each week. This
needs to be available at the adjourned hearing.

The case has been adjourned to the week commencing 12” December 2005
for Sentence.

If there is to be a claim for investigation costs please could this be provided
as soon as possible.

Regarding the loss Counsel seeks instruction as to whether this should be
recovered by way of a compensation order or whether there is to be an
application for confiscation under POCA. Could you please advise me of
your views as a matter of urgency.
