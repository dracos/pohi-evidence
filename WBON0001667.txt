CONSENT ORDER

UPON the Defendant in these proceedings having been a Claimant in the Post Office Group
Litigation against Post Office Limited (Queens Bench Division Claim Numbers HQ16X01238,
HQ17X02637 & HQ17X04248) (‘the Group Litigation’).

AND UPON the Group Litigation having been managed by way of a Group Litigation Order
dated 22 March 2017, and that order having now been discharged pursuant to a confidential
settlement agreement reached between the parties to the Group Litigation dated 10 December
2019 (‘the Settlement Agreement’).

Agreement in respect of these proceedings.

AND UPON the parties affirming and seeking to give effect to the Settlement Agreement.

BY CONSENT, IT IS ORDERED THAT: -
1. The Claimant's claim against the Defendant in these proceedings is hereby dismissed.

2. The Claimant and Defendant waive any and all rights to enforce any and all outstanding
orders (including those relating to costs) made in these proceedings.

3. There shall be no order as to costs.
