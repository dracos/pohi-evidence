SECRETARY OF STATE’S MEETING WITH NEVILLE BAIN,
CHAIRMAN OF THE POST OFFICE AT 1640 ON THURSDAY 4 MARCH

ils You are meeting with Neville Bain, Chairman, and John Roberts, Chief
Executive of the Post Office at 1640 on Thursday 4 March. Martyn Baker, Judy
Britton and David Sibbick will attend the meeting.

Objective of Meeti

’s To assure the Post Office that you are committed to the reform
programme and to discuss any concerns the Post Office have on the overall
reform package and on the White Paper in particular.

Background

3. There has been ongoing dialogue between Ian McCartney, Neville Bain
and John Roberts. On 21 January they met to discuss major issues including
progress on implementing the review reform package. Ian McCartney also
recently met the new non-Executive Board members and the Board of the Post
Office on 15 February. Officials have also been working up the White Paper
in close consultation with Post Office senior management.
