10 INTRODUCTION

This document contains a definition listing, by postal zones, of the class of service
delivered to PO branches under the service category of Time to Repair.

2.0 SCOPE

This defines the following:

a) Postal zone locations classified as being Remote

b) Postal zone locations classified as being Intermediate
All other postal zones are classified as being Local for the purpose of Time to Repair
service performance.
