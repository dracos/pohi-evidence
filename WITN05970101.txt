Objectives of the Demonstrator Stream

The objectives of the demonstrator stream are:

Requirements

e to clarify the requirements with the three suppliers
and ensure that they have a valid understanding of
these requirements

e to identify deficiencies in the requirements and to
feed these back to the BA/POCL requirements team for
resolution

Solutions

e to explore and understand the supplier’s solutions
and to identify issues and risks associated with
their solutions (input to Service Provider Risk
Register)

e to provide a process to allow suppliers to reduce
their Risks

Assessment

e to assess the supplier’s solutions as input to the
overall Evaluation process

e to provide confidence to the stakeholders in the
supplier’s solutions
