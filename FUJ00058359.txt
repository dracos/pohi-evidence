1 INTRODUCTION

This weekly report summarises the progress of the implementation of the
Horizon Live Trial into post offices in the South Wales South West region.
The purpose of this report is to provide the POCL region with a summary
of the progress to date against the planned activity in this region; a
summary of the planned activities in the following week; outstanding
sundry activities; and general issues that may arise.

2 SCOPE

This report only covers the implementation activities for this phase within
the region and is not intended to include any reporting requirements for
outlets in steady state within the region.
