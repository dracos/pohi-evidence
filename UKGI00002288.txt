Subject: Jo Swinson transition rom maternity leave - plus commission to directorates

Dear All

An update on some plans in train to support Jo Swinson's return from maternity leave. We expect Jo
to return to BIS / GEO around the end of June - but will confirm this once definitive. Jenny will
continue as Minister, taking all decisions, until Jo's return.

Briefing Jo

We are starting to brief Jo more regularly on some of the developments since her maternity leave.
We will begin this in May through catch ups between Jo and Jenny, as well as with private office.

We then propose to do some high level one-hour briefing sessions between Jo and each of her main
directorates in early June: for Labour Market; Business Environment; Post Office; Insolvency Service;
CCP and GEO. Jenny will not attend these sessions. It will be an opportunity for you to brief Jo
directly on the main developments in your areas since her maternity leave, as well as look ahead to
the rest of 2014 (including particular decisions Jo may need to make shortly after her return). The
recent strategy sessions you've had with Jenny may be a good starting point for this briefing. Jo is
currently free on Monday 2nd and Tuesday 3rd June. Lauren (our new diary secretary) will send
some provisional briefing slots to each directorate. If you have any particular timing requests, please
do let Lauren know and we'll try to accommodate this. For any sessions we do not manage to fit into
these days, we will look to do them shortly after.

Following this, we are working with Jo to see what dates she has for the rest of June for further
briefing sessions as necessary. Please do highlight to the relevant PS in our office if you feel
additional briefing sessions after these high-level ones would be particularly valuable. For example,
Jo will certainly need some briefing on the Small Business Bill.
