1. PURPOSE.

It is important that Investigators, when conducting both witness and suspect interviews, in the course
of criminal investigations know the necessary points to prove for offence(s) they are investigating and
that they factor these into the planning and preparation stage of the interviewing process. Below are
details of the points to prove for the more common offences that RMG Ltd Investigators may investigate.

Advice should be sought from the Criminal Law Team in the event that an offence not readily
identifiable is to be considered.
