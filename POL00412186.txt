Annual performance discussion: Non-Executive Directors

Name: Chairman :

1 I How have you contributed to Board discussions and decisions?

2. I How have you contributed [chaired where applicable] to Committee discussions and decisions?

3 I What are the mains skills and experience you bring to the Board?

4 I How would you assess your contribution beyond the Boardroom?

5 I How would you assess effectiveness of relationships with fellow Board members, senior management and stakeholders (I will also speak with other directors)?

6 I Is there anything you would choose to do more of or less of to increase your effectiveness as a Non-Executive Director?

7 I Are you confident that you have sufficient time to dedicate to the role?

8 I Do you have any training and development requirements or areas of the business on which you require further briefing?

9 I Anything else?
