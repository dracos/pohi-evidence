For the year ending 31 March 2019, the Departmental Group
incurred a spend of £12.1 billion.

Of this £1.4 billion related to the core Department and agencies,
and £10.7 billion related to our partner organisations.

The diagram on the left shows the major areas of spend. The
majority of the department's spend, and policy delivery is
through it’s Partner Organisations, including:

+ £7,625 million by UK Research and Innovation (UKRI), on
funding for Science and Research.

+ £2,200 million by the Nuclear Decommissioning Authority
(NDA) on managing our energy legacy safely and
responsibly.

+ £347 million by UK Space Agency (UKSA) on delivering an
excellent space programme with the maximum economic,
scientific and policy benefit for the UK.

+ £208 million by the British Business Bank (BBB) on
supporting small businesses in the UK at all stages of their
development.

Note: This expenditure corresponds to the net total DEL expenditure
for our Departmental Group. It excludes AME expenditure which
represents volatile, demand-led spend and technical accounting
matters.
