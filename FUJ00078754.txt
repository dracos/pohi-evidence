1 Introduction

This document comprises the Acceptance Pack for the Pathway-to-TIP
Interface Acceptance Review. This document will be accompanied by copies
of the documents that have been declared for this segment of Acceptance
Review as identified in ref.(1), the Pathway-to-TIP Interface Acceptance
Reviews Plan.

2 Scope

The documents for review are shown in section 3 together with a note as to
those Acceptance Criteria which reference the document. It should be noted
that the Authorities are not being asked to conduct a document review. The
purpose of this phase is for the Authorities to assure themselves that the
statements made in the various Acceptance Review documents are sufficient
to meet each Acceptance Criterion as stated in ref.(2), the Pathway-to-TIP
Interface Acceptance Specification.
