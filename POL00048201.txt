REGINA v HUGHIE NOEL THOMAS
CAERNARFON CROWN COURT

ADJOURNED SENTENCE - 20'" OCTOBER 2006
CASE NO: POLTD/0506/0401

The above named Defendant appeared at Caernarfon Crown Court on the
29" September where he pleaded Guilty to one offence of false accounting. This
was pursuant to a Basis of Plea which makes it clear that no blame was
attributed to the Horizon Computer System.

The Defendant accepted that there was a shortage but he could not explain how
it came about. He accepted that as a Subpostmaster he is contractually obliged
to make good the shortage.

The next hearing is on the 20" October 2006.
